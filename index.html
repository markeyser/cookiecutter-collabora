Welcome to Cookiecutter Two Lanes Documentation

Cookiecutter Two Lanes is a specialized Cookiecutter template designed to facilitate collaborative artificial intelligence, machine learning, and generative AI projects. This template serves as an extensive framework, bringing together a comprehensive set of tools and practices that meet the evolving demands of modern AI development.

Purpose and Vision

Our goal is to establish a standardized foundation for your AI projects, akin to two lanes moving forward in harmony, to guarantee both diversity and uniformity from the beginning. The Cookiecutter Two Lanes template integrates best practices for collaboration and reproducibility, as advocated in "The Turing Way," promoting an open culture of data science and research within the AI community.

The Cookiecutter Two Lanes template is crafted for ML professionals who aspire to engage in collaborative and reproducible research methodologies. It lays the groundwork for projects to be conducted with efficiency and precision, providing a robust starting point for innovation and discovery in the field of AI.

  • Configure the working environment
    Configure your data science project's working environment according to the Explanation documentation.

  • Learn how to use tools
    How-To Guides teach you how to produce professional code and documentation using the tools available in your working environment.

  • Step-by-step guide
    Tutorials demonstrate how to use the tools in your working environment to complete an end-to-end toy data science project.

  • Reference
    Reference material, such as naming conventions for Data Science items, can be found in the API Reference section.
